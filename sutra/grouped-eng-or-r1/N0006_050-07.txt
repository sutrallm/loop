7. Non-Heedlessness (Subduing Desire)  

[69–78] 1–10  

Summary Verses:  

The Tathagata and footprints,  
roof and root-core,  
summer-born, flower, king, moon,  
sun, robe—these ten phrases.  

Regarding the chapter on non-heedlessness, the subjugation of desire in accordance with the power of exertion may be expounded in detail.
