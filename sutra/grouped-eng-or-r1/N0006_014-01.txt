1. The Section on Various Elements  
1. 1. The First: The Elements  
1. 1. At that time, the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī.  
1. 2. The Blessed One said: "Bhikkhus, I will teach you about the various elements. Listen carefully and attend closely. I will speak." The bhikkhus replied: "Yes, Venerable One."  
1. 3. The Blessed One said: "Bhikkhus, what are the various elements?"  
1. 4. The eye element, the form element, the eye-consciousness element; the ear element, the sound element, the ear-consciousness element; the nose element, the odor element, the nose-consciousness element; the tongue element, the taste element, the tongue-consciousness element; the body element, the tactile element, the body-consciousness element; the mind element, the mental-phenomena element, the mind-consciousness element. These, bhikkhus, are called the various elements.  
2. 2. The Second: Contact  
2. 1. At that time, the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī.  
2. 2. The Blessed One said: "Bhikkhus, from various elements arise various contacts."  
2. 3. "Bhikkhus, what are the various elements?"  
2. 4. The eye element, ear element, nose element, tongue element, body element, mind element. These, bhikkhus, are called the various elements.  
2. 5. "Bhikkhus, how does it happen that from various elements, various contacts arise?"  
2. 6. Bhikkhus, dependent on the eye element, eye-contact arises; dependent on the ear element... nose element... tongue element... body element... mind element, mind-contact arises.  
2. 7. "Thus, bhikkhus, from various elements arise various contacts."  
3. 3. The Third: Not This  
3. 1. At that time, the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī.  
3. 2. The Blessed One said: "Bhikkhus, dependent on various elements, various contacts arise; dependent on various contacts, elements do not arise.  
3. 3. Bhikkhus, what are the various elements?  
3. 4. The eye element... mind element. These, bhikkhus, are called the various elements.  
3. 5. Bhikkhus, how is it that dependent on various elements, various contacts arise, but dependent on various contacts, elements do not arise?"  
3. 6. Bhikkhus, dependent on the eye element, eye-contact arises; dependent on eye-contact, the eye element does not arise... Dependent on the mind element, mind-contact arises; dependent on mind-contact, the mind element does not arise.  
3. 7. "Thus, bhikkhus, dependent on various elements, various contacts arise, but dependent on various contacts, elements do not arise."  
4. 4. The Fourth: Feeling (1)  
4. 1. At that time, the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī.  
4. 2. The Blessed One said: "Bhikkhus, dependent on various elements, various contacts arise; dependent on various contacts, various feelings arise."  
4. 3. "Bhikkhus, what are the various elements?"  
4. 4. The eye element... mind element. These, bhikkhus, are called the various elements.  
4. 5. "Bhikkhus, how does it happen that dependent on various elements, various contacts arise, and dependent on various contacts, various feelings arise?"  
4. 6. Bhikkhus, dependent on the eye element, eye-contact arises; dependent on eye-contact, the feeling born of eye-contact arises... Dependent on the mind element, mind-contact arises; dependent on mind-contact, the feeling born of mind-contact arises.  
4. 7. "Thus, bhikkhus, dependent on various elements, various contacts arise; dependent on various contacts, various feelings arise."  
5. 5. The Fifth: Feeling (2)  
5. 1. At that time, the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī.  
5. 2. The Blessed One said: "Bhikkhus, dependent on various elements, various contacts arise; dependent on various contacts, various feelings arise. Dependent on various feelings, contacts do not arise; dependent on various contacts, elements do not arise."  
5. 3. "Bhikkhus, what are the various elements? The eye element... mind element. These, bhikkhus, are called the various elements."  
5. 4. "Bhikkhus, how is it that dependent on various elements, various contacts arise... but dependent on various feelings, contacts do not arise, and dependent on various contacts, elements do not arise?"  
5. 5-9. Dependent on the eye element, eye-contact arises... The feeling born of eye-contact does not give rise to eye-contact; dependent on eye-contact, the eye element does not arise...  
5. 10. Dependent on the mind element, mind-contact arises... The feeling born of mind-contact does not give rise to mind-contact; dependent on mind-contact, the mind element does not arise.  
5. 11. "Thus, bhikkhus, dependent on various elements, various contacts arise... and dependent on various contacts, elements do not arise."  
6. 6. The Sixth: Elements  
6. 1. At that time, the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī.  
6. 2. The Blessed One said: "Bhikkhus, I will teach you about various elements. Listen closely..."  
6. 3. "Bhikkhus, what are the various elements? The form element, sound element, odor element, taste element, tactile element, mental-phenomena element."  
6. 4. "These, bhikkhus, are called the various elements."  
7. 7. The Seventh: Perceptions  
7. 1. At that time, the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī.  
7. 2. The Blessed One said: "Bhikkhus, dependent on various elements, various perceptions arise; dependent on various perceptions, various intentions arise; dependent on various intentions, various desires arise; dependent on various desires, various passions arise; dependent on various passions, various quests arise."  
7. 3. "Bhikkhus, what are the various elements? The form element... mental-phenomena element. These, bhikkhus, are called the various elements."  
7. 4. "How is it that dependent on various elements... various quests arise?"  
7. 5-9. Dependent on the form element, form-perception arises... form-quest...  
7. 10. Dependent on the mental-phenomena element, mental-phenomena perception arises... mental-phenomena quest.  
7. 11. "Thus, bhikkhus, dependent on various elements... various quests arise."  
8. 8. The Eighth: Not This  
8. 1. At that time, the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī.  
8. 2. The Blessed One said: "Bhikkhus, dependent on various elements, various perceptions arise... quests arise.  
8. 3. Dependent on various quests, passions do not arise; dependent on various passions, desires do not arise; dependent on various desires, intentions do not arise; dependent on various intentions, perceptions do not arise; dependent on various perceptions, elements do not arise.  
8. 4-9. "Bhikkhus, what are the various elements? The form element... mental-phenomena element. These, bhikkhus, are called the various elements."  
8. 10. "How is it that dependent on various elements... elements do not arise?"  
8. 11. Dependent on the form element... form-quest does not give rise to form-passion... form-element.  
8. 12-16. Similarly for sound... mental-phenomena.  
8. 17. "Thus, bhikkhus, dependent on various elements... elements do not arise."  
9. 9. The Ninth: Contact (1)  
9. 1. At that time, the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī.  
9. 2. The Blessed One said: "Bhikkhus, dependent on various elements, various perceptions arise... quests arise; dependent on various quests, various acquisitions arise."  
9. 3. "Bhikkhus, what are the various elements... mental-phenomena element. These are called the various elements."  
9. 4. "How does it happen?"  
9. 5-10. Dependent on the form element... form-acquisition... Similarly for sound... mental-phenomena.  
9. 11. "Thus, bhikkhus, dependent on various elements... various acquisitions arise."  
10. 10. The Tenth: Contact (2)  
10. 1. At that time, the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī.  
10. 2. The Blessed One said: "Bhikkhus, dependent on various elements... quests, acquisitions. Dependent on acquisitions, quests do not arise... elements do not arise."  
10. 3. "Bhikkhus, what are the various elements... mental-phenomena element."  
10. 4-9. "How is it?"  
10. 10. Mental-phenomena... do not give rise to the mental-phenomena element.  
10. 11. "Thus, bhikkhus... elements do not arise."  
Verses:  
The sections on elements, contact, what is not,  
Two on feeling, making five internal matters;  
Elements, perceptions, what is not,  
Two on contact, completing five external matters.
