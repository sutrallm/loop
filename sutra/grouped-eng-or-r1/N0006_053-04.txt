4. Chapter on Seeking  
35.–44. 1.–10.  
Bhikkhus, there are three kinds of seeking. What are the three? They are the seeking of sensuality, the seeking of existence, and the seeking of the holy life... and so on...  
The summary verse states:  
Seeking, sequence, taints;  
Existence and the three sufferings;  
Hindrances, impurities, relinquishment;  
Feelings and two kinds of craving.
