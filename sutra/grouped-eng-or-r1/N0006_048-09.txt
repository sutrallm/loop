9. Chapter on Heedfulness (Distance and Support)  

83.-92. 1-10  

Verses summary say:  

The Thus Come One and footprints,  
Rooftop, root and core,  
Summer-born, flower king, moon,  
Sun, robe, and other ten phrases.  

Extensive discourse on distance and support, based on the roots, extensively explains the chapter on heedfulness.
