1.2. The Chapter on Searchings (Detachment)  
1.101–110 First–Twelfth  
1.  
Bhikkhus! There are three kinds of searchings. What are the three? The search for sensual pleasures, the search for existence, and the search for a holy life... thus it is extensively explained...  
Summary Verse:  
Searching, sequence, taints,  
Existence, suffering, hindrances, defilements,  
Agitation, feeling, and the two cravings.
