7. Search Chapter  
[67–76] 1–10  

2.  

Bhikkhus, there are three kinds of searches. What are the three kinds of searches? ... up to ... These may be expounded in detail.  

Verses of Summary:  
Searches in sequence, taints,  
existence, two kinds of suffering,  
hindrances, stains, renunciation,  
feelings, and up to craving.  

Based on the bases of psychic power, the search may be expanded in detail.
