5. The Chapter on Torrents (Dependent on Detachment)  

[47-55] 1-9  

[56] 10. The Higher Fetters  

2.  
"Bhikkhus, there are five higher fetters. What are the five higher fetters? Desire for form, desire for the formless, conceit, restlessness, and ignorance. Bhikkhus, these are the five higher fetters.  

Bhikkhus, to fully understand, thoroughly know, completely eradicate, and abandon these five higher fetters, one should cultivate the five powers. What are the five powers?  

3.  
Bhikkhus, here a bhikkhu cultivates the power of faith directed toward relinquishment through detachment, dispassion, and cessation... cultivates the power of effort... the power of mindfulness... the power of concentration... the power of wisdom.  

Bhikkhus, to fully understand, thoroughly know, completely eradicate, and abandon these five higher fetters, one should cultivate these five powers."  

The summary verses state:  
Torrents, yokes, grasping, bonds, latent tendencies, sense pleasures up to hindrances, aggregates, lower and higher fetters.  

The Chapter on Torrents can be elaborated in accordance with the powers.
