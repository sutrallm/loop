2. The Chapter on Diligence (Detachment Preserved)  
[13–22] 1–10  
Summary verse:  
The Tathāgata and footprints,  
Roofs and root-essences,  
Summer-born flowers, king, moon,  
Sun, robe, and ten verses.  

Regarding the Chapter on Diligence, it can be elaborated in accordance with the power of dependence.
