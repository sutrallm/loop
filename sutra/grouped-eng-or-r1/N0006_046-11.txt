11. The Work of Strength (Detachment)  

[99–100] 1–12  

2.  

Bhikkhus, just as all works requiring strength are done depending on the earth, established on the earth, so too all works requiring strength... and so it is extensively explained...  

Verses of Summary:  
Strength, seeds, nāgas, trees, pots, up to thorns,  
sky, two clouds, ships, travelers, rivers.
